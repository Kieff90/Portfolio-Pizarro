How it works

01
Discovery call (30 min, free)
We talk about your AI delivery challenge: what you're building, where you're stuck, and what success looks like. No commitment required.
02
Scoping & proposal
I send a written proposal within 48 hours: scope, deliverables, timeline, and engagement type (fractional / project / advisory). No generic decks.
03
Kickoff & delivery
We align on communication rhythms, tools, and success criteria. I integrate with your team and start delivering immediately, without a lengthy onboarding phase.
04
Review & close
At the end of each engagement I provide a written handover: process documentation, metrics, and recommendations. Your team owns everything.
